# Stella Mobiles - Design System & Color Palette

## 1. Core Philosophy
The Stella Mobiles design language is built on a foundation of premium elegance, high contrast, and structural depth. The design strictly avoids harsh neon glows and jarring colors (specifically, all blues and cyans are prohibited), opting instead for a clean, matte, and luxurious aesthetic.

## 2. Color Palette

### Base Colors (Structure & Depth)
*   **Deep Black** (`#050505` or `bg-black`): Used for the primary app background and outer canvases. It provides the canvas for high-contrast elements to pop.
*   **Dark Charcoal Grey** (`#1A1A1A` to `#242424`): Used for product cards, sidebars, navigation bars, and structural elements. This creates necessary visual depth and hierarchy against the Deep Black background.
*   **Crisp White** (`#FFFFFF` to `#F3F4F6`): Used for all primary typography, headings, and crucial icons to ensure maximum readability and contrast.
*   **Subtle Grey** (`#9CA3AF`): Used for secondary text, muted descriptions, and inactive states.

### Accent Colors (Interaction & Highlighting)
*   **Stella Matte Red** (e.g., `#E63946` or Tailwind `red-600`): The primary brand accent. Used for the 'T' in the Stella logo, primary "Add to Cart" or "Buy Now" buttons, active state highlights, and crucial call-to-actions. Must remain a clean, matte finish without harsh neon glowing effects.
*   **Warm Amber / Gold** (e.g., `#F59E0B` or Tailwind `amber-500`): The secondary luxury accent. Used sparingly to denote premium quality, such as 5-star product ratings, "Exclusive" or "Pro" badges, and special deal highlights.

### Prohibited Colors
*   **Blue & Cyan:** Absolutely no shades of blue, cyan, or teal are to be used in the UI.

## 3. Typography & Styling
*   **Typography:** Modern, clean sans-serif fonts (e.g., Inter, Montserrat, or Outfit) to maintain a highly professional look.
*   **Glassmorphism:** Elegant, subtle grayscale glassmorphism can be used for overlapping elements (like sticky headers or floating filter bars), ensuring it blends smoothly with the charcoal and black backgrounds.
